Buy A Ticket

The farebox at the front of the bus accepts cash fares and passes but does not provide change. Please have the exact fare ready, as bus operators cannot handle money or make change. Pennies and foreign objects are not accepted.

  • Online: Visit https://www.go901transit.com/ or the MATA Go901 mobile app to buy your ticket. Choose your ticket type, enter your payment details, and you’ll receive an e-ticket instantly that you can show directly from your phone or email when boarding.
  • At the Bus Station: Visit the ticket counter at your nearest bus station. The staff will assist you in selecting your route and ticket type, and you can pay using cash, credit, or debit card.
  • Onboard: Some bus services allow you to purchase tickets directly from the driver, usually with cash. However, it’s recommended to buy your ticket in advance to avoid delays.

    • MATA offers reduced fares for students (grades 1-12), senior citizens (65+), and individuals with disabilities on fixed route buses and trolleys. A valid MATA ID card is required for eligibility, which costs $3 and can be processed at the William Hudson Transit Center (444 North Main St). Replacement cards are $5. Student Fare: Proof of enrollment and ID (e.g., birth certificate, social security card) required. Senior Fare: Individuals 65+ pay half the base fare with ID proof. Medicare Card Holders: Fares are half the base fare for fixed routes. Disability Fare: Individuals with disabilities pay half the base fare. A Medicare card or a physician’s verification is required. All passes are non-refundable, non-replaceable, and non-exchangeable. For more info, call 901.523.8134.
